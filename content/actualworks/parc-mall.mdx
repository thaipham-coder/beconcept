Challenge
District 8 is an area that is gradually forming an urban face in the context of modernization. Therefore, the project has the stature of changing shopping habits and experiencing new spaces for residents 

Solution
AVT focuses on shaping an Urban Shopping Mall filled with Timely information on daily life and Seasonal fashion creation for affluent Lifestyles in the future. A place for people to meet the city and people as the creation of a new Community base.
